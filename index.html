Overview

Live the Godrej Life at Yelahanka, North Bengaluru

Godrej Properties is set to unveil an upcoming premium residential enclave in Yelahanka, North Bengaluru. Spanning 10 acres, the project comprises nearly 800 thoughtfully designed homes across 9 elegant high-rise towers (2B + G + 15 floors).

Ideally located opposite the Oracle office campus, the development offers bright, well-ventilated residences with dual balconies and dedicated utility spaces. Residents will enjoy a host of world-class amenities, including a modern gymnasium and a swimming pool, crafted for a refined urban lifestyle.

With possession planned in approximately 3 years, homebuyers can choose from a range of spacious configurations: 3 BHK Premium, 3 BHK Luxe, 3.5 BHK Luxe with staff quarters, and 4.5 BHK Luxe with staff quarters—perfectly blending comfort, luxury, and convenience.

About Developer

Godrej Properties is one of India’s leading real estate developers, renowned for innovation, sustainability, and quality. Established in 1990 as the real estate arm of the Godrej Group, the company has reshaped urban living through premium residential, commercial, and township developments. Under the leadership of Pirojsha Godrej, it seamlessly blends modern design with eco-friendly practices, earning trust across major cities such as Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore, Chennai, and Delhi-NCR.

With over 250+ projects delivered nationwide, notable developments include Godrej Woods (Noida), Godrej Platinum (Bangalore), Godrej Air (Gurgaon), and Godrej Eternity (Bangalore). Recognized with prestigious awards like “Best Real Estate Brand” by The Economic Times and the “Green Champion Award” by IGBC, the company continues to focus on sustainable growth, smart townships, and luxury residential projects across India.
